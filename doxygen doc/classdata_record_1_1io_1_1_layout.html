Detailed Description

This class do the layout.

In others words it take the entire uml diagram and move each element to a better position, for exemple it will take every interfaces and put them on the top of the window and put the classes implementing those interfaces in the bottom of the window. It also does a change in the relationship lines: they are not straight but with angles of 90° for a better understanding.
